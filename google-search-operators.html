" "

Surround your search term with quotation marks to get an exact match of that word or phrase.

"B2B marketing jobs"

*

The asterisk, known as a wildcard, searches for any word or phrase you include.

terriers * labradors

OR o |

Place OR (all caps) between two words to combine searches. Use it to search for results that have one of those words but not both.

B2B OR B2C
B2B | B2C

AND

Place AND (all caps) between two words if you want your results to include both.

B2B AND B2C

( )

Put a search term between parenthesis to group commands.

(B2B OR B2C) marketing jobs

-

Put a hyphen before any term you want to exclude from results.

dog breeds -terriers

..

If you want to search for a number between a range of numbers, place two periods between that range.

election results 1996..2016

$

Use a dollar sign to search for prices. You can use the Euro sign to search for prices in that currency.

piano $1500

@

Use this symbol to search for results in social media.

@twitter

#

Use this symbol to search for hashtags.

#sundayfunday

in

Put "in" between two units you want to convert.

100 F in C

site:

Search within a certain website or web domain.

site:indeed.com

related:

Find websites with content similar to a certain website.

related:indeed.com

cache:

Find Google's most recent cached (saved) version of a webpage.

cache:indeed.com

filetype:

Search for only one type of file.

filetype:PDF

define:

Find the definition of a word.

define:neuroscience

weather:

Find the weather for a location by city, state, county or ZIP code.

weather:77354

stocks:

Get information about a stock, including its price and recent trends

stocks:TSLA

map:

See a map of a location.

map:houston

AROUND(X)

Search for terms within a certain number of words of each other.

marketing AROUND(5) B2B

intext:

Search for words within a webpage's body text.

intext:B2B

allintext:

Search a page for every term after the command. Add "all" before any of the following search operators (allinurl, allintitle, allinposttitle, allinanchor) to search for all terms.

allintext:B2B marketing jobs

inurl:

Find a URL with a certain word or phrase.

dog breeds inurl:terrier

intitle:

Search a page title for a word or phrase.

intitle:backsplash

inposttitle:

Search for a term within a blog post title.

inposttitle:DIY backsplash

inanchor:

Search for sites linked with specific anchor text.

inanchor:backsplash
